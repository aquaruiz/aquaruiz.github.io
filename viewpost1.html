The humanoid robot Sophia comes to Bulgaria

The robot has attracted attention all over the world - Sophia, who, jokingly or not, said it would destroy people, and then became the first humanoid machine that had been granted citizenship and declared its desire to create a generation. This year Sophia is coming to Bulgaria to take part in the business conference about the business opportunities and challenges. The forum will be held on March 15, 2018.

Sofia is considered to be the most modern robot, endowed with a remarkable ability to express and communicate. She can simulate a full range of expressions and recognize the emotions of her interlocutors, and look them into eyes and talks deeply with them. For the second time Sofia will come in June as one of the official guests of Webit.Feestival 2018.
